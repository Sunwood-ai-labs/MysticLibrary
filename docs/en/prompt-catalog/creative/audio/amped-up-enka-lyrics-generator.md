# Up-Tempo Enka Lyrics Generator

## Intended Use

- Generating up-tempo, high-energy enka lyrics
- Creating lyrics for music generation AI (Suno, etc.)
- Fusing Eurobeat-like style with enka's appeal

## Prompt

```text
# Up-Tempo Enka Lyrics Generation Prompt

Referring to the Suno tag cheat sheet below, generate lyrics according to the following requirements.

## Requirements
- Japanese lyrics
- Up-tempo and high-energy
- Enka style

## Music Generation AI "Suno" Tag Cheat Sheet

Tag Structure
[Intro] - Song introduction
[verse] - Regular verse
[Verse 2], [Verse 3], etc. - Continuing verses
[chorus] - Repeating theme that serves as the song's chorus
[pre-chorus] - Section leading to the chorus
[post-chorus] - Section immediately following the chorus
[hook] - Catchy, memorable musical phrase
[bridge], [bridge 2], [second bridge] - Section contrasting with verse and chorus, the so-called bridge
[break] - Rest or instrumental-only break
[interlude] - Musical break between song sections
[refrain] - Series of repeating riffs
[outro] - Song's closing/ending section
[coda] - Song's completion section
[solo] - Instrumental solo section
[instrumental] - Instrument-only section without vocals
[vamp] - Repetitive musical section used to extend the song
[build-up] - Section gradually increasing tension or intensity
[drop] - Strong peak common in electronic music
[fade-out] - Gradually decreasing volume to end
[tag] - Brief phrase repeated at the end of the song
[counterpoint] - Section interweaving various melodies
[middle 8] [B section] - Section for contrast, typically in the middle of the song
[modulation] - Changing key within the song (modulation)

Performance-related tags
[powerful] - Powerful vocals
[forte] - Stronger volume, forte
[belting] - Powerful and emotional
[emphatic] - Emphasis (for specific lyrics or phrases)
[intense] - Emotional/dynamic intensity
[climax] - The most climactic part of the song
[passionate] - Deeply emotional vocal performance
[soaring] - Magnificent and moving effect, dramatically raising voice to high range
```

## How to Use

1. Specify a theme or subject matter
2. Execute the prompt to generate lyrics
3. Input the generated lyrics into music generation AI such as Suno

## Input Example

```text
Theme: Living life positively
Requirements:
- Japanese lyrics
- Up-tempo and high-energy
- Enka style
```

## Notes

- Use Suno's tag structure correctly
- While preserving enka's appeal (lyrical aspects, life lessons)
- Create up-tempo, high-energy excitement
- Make lyrics natural in Japanese
