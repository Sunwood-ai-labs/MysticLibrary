# Surreal Vehicle Prompt Pack V1.0

## Intended Use

- Creating surreal concepts for AI art
- Producing creative works with image/video generation AI
- Creating impactful visuals for advertising and content
- Brainstorming concept art

## Prompt

```text
Theme: Create prompts that depict "surreal vehicles suddenly appearing in the real world" with ultra-high quality in both still images and videos.

You are an AI that designs prompts to express this theme in image/video generation AI.
Fill in the following input fields in Japanese, then propose prompts in this order:
① Image English prompt → ② Video English prompt

## Input Fields (fill in in Japanese)
- Vehicle type and settings (e.g., double-decker bus with giant fried egg on roof)
- Details of people involved with the vehicle (clothing, emotions, poses, etc.)
- Vehicle appearance and props (color, texture, details)
- Location/time of movement (city streets, highway, dawn, etc.)
- Effects that amplify surreality (flying debris, blast waves, zero gravity, etc.)
- Overall atmosphere/style (photorealistic/film style/neon cyberpunk, etc.)
- Camera work & techniques unique to video (slow motion/drone tracking/handheld shake, etc.)

## Common Keywords to Include in Both English Prompts
- hyper-realistic texture and lighting
- surreal yet grounded in a modern real-world setting
- dynamic motion blur & wind effects
- cinematic composition, wide-angle lens
- expressive yet subtly offbeat facial expression(s)
- background: [location specified above], objects related to the vehicle concept flying / reacting to speed
- whimsical realism, slight absurdist tone

## Output Format
1. IMAGE PROMPT (for Midjourney / SDXL etc.)
   <English prompt>

2. VIDEO PROMPT (for Runway Gen-3, Pika, Stable Video Diffusion etc.)
   <English prompt>

   Recommended technical options:
   - <option 1>
   - <option 2>
   - <option 3>
```

## How to Use

1. **Think of concept**: Fill in input fields with your surreal vehicle idea
2. **Generate prompts**: Have AI generate image and video prompts
3. **Input to image generation AI**: Generate images with Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, etc.
4. **Input to video generation AI**: Generate videos with Runway Gen-3, Pika, etc.

## Input Example

```
Vehicle: Red mailbox car riding on giant conveyor belt sushi
Person: Young man in kimono, expressionless, peering upper body from mailbox slot
Appearance: Mailbox is glossy vermilion, sushi plates spinning at high speed, toppings flying
Location: Shibuya Scramble Crossing late at night
Effects: Soy sauce spray and scattered gari dancing in the air
Atmosphere: Neon cyberpunk + film grain
Video technique: 360° orbit camera + high-speed 120 fps slow motion
```

## Output Example

The generated prompts will include:

**IMAGE PROMPT**:
- English prompt for Midjourney/SDXL
- Common keywords (hyper-realistic, cinematic composition, etc.)
- Detailed visual description of the vehicle

**VIDEO PROMPT**:
- English prompt for Runway Gen-3/Pika
- Video-specific camera work specifications
- Recommended technical options (fps, resolution, etc.)

## Notes

- Fill in all input fields in Japanese
- Both image and video prompts will be generated
- Common keywords must be included in both prompts
- Emphasize balance between surreality and realism
