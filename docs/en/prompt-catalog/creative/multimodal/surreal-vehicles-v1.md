# Reality Cross Surreal Vehicles Batch Generation Prompt V1.0

## Intended Use

- Generate images and videos of surreal vehicles placed in real-world settings
- Use with AI tools like Midjourney, SDXL, Runway Gen-3
- Aim for hyper-realistic textures and cinematic composition

## Prompt Text

See the Japanese version of this document for the complete prompt template.

The prompt template includes:

1. **Theme**: Create prompts depicting "surreal vehicles suddenly appearing in the real world"
2. **Input Fields**: Vehicle type, people details, appearance, location, effects, atmosphere, camera work
3. **Common Keywords**: hyper-realistic texture, surreal yet grounded, dynamic motion blur, cinematic composition
4. **Output Format**: Image prompt (Midjourney/SDXL) and Video prompt (Runway Gen-3/Pika)

### Input Fields
- **Vehicle Type and Setting** (Example: A double-decker bus with a giant fried egg on the roof)
- **Details of People Involved with the Vehicle** (Clothing, emotions, poses, etc.)
- **Vehicle Appearance and Props** (Color, texture, details)
- **Location/Time of Movement** (City center, highway, dawn, etc.)
- **Effects that Amplify Surrealism** (Flying debris, blast waves, zero gravity, etc.)
- **Overall Atmosphere/Style** (Photorealistic/Film tone/Neon cyberpunk, etc.)
- **Camera Work and Techniques Unique to Videos** (Slow motion/drone tracking/handheld shake, etc.)

### Common Keywords
- **hyper-realistic texture and lighting**
- **surreal yet grounded in a modern real-world setting**
- **dynamic motion blur and wind effects**
- **cinematic composition, wide-angle lens**
- **expressive yet subtly offbeat facial expression(s)**
- **background: [location specified above], objects related to the vehicle concept flying / reacting to speed**
- **whimsical realism, slight absurdist tone**

## Usage

1. Fill in the input fields with desired vehicle and settings
2. AI automatically generates English prompts for both image and video
3. Input generated prompts into respective AI tools

## Input Example

```
Vehicle: Giant floating donut as cockpit aircraft
Person: Female pilot in helmet, relaxed expression
Appearance: Pink glaze with scattered sprinkles
Location: Golden Gate Bridge, San Francisco, at sunset
Effects: Sugar powder dancing in wind, rainbow-colored trail
Atmosphere: Photorealistic + fantasy harmony
Video Tech: Drone lateral tracking transitioning to rapid ascent
```

## Output Example

```
IMAGE PROMPT:
A floating donut piloted aircraft with pink glaze and scattered sprinkles, female pilot in helmet looking relaxed, Golden Gate Bridge backdrop at sunset, hyper-realistic texture and lighting, surreal yet grounded in modern real-world setting, cinematic composition wide-angle lens, whimsical realism slight absurdist tone --ar 16:9 --v 6.0

VIDEO PROMPT:
A pink glazed donut aircraft soaring over Golden Gate Bridge at sunset, female pilot visible through transparent cockpit, sprinkles trailing behind like rainbow dust, drone tracking shot transitioning to rapid ascent, 120fps slow motion, hyper-realistic texture and lighting, dynamic motion blur and wind effects, cinematic composition
```

## Notes

- Common keywords must be included in both prompts
- Specify camera work techniques specifically in video prompts
- Emphasize balance between realistic quality and surrealism
