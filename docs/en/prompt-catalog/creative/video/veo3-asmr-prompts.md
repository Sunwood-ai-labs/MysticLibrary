# Veo3 ASMR Video Generation Prompts

## Intended Use

- Creating ASMR videos with AI video generation tools (Veo3, etc.)
- Producing relaxing content with a serene Japanese atmosphere
- Generating beautiful visual content utilizing miniature kimono figures
- Creating art content that expresses the fusion of traditional culture and modern technology

## Prompt

This prompt supports three scene patterns. Select the scene you want to generate.

### Pattern 1: Shodo (Japanese Calligraphy) ASMR

```yaml
scene_description: "A quiet and beautiful scene of a miniature kimono figure performing Japanese calligraphy"

subject:
  type: "Approximately 15cm miniature kimono figure"
  material: "High-quality PVC, exquisitely crafted down to details"
  appearance: "Elegant female figure wearing beautiful silk-style kimono"
  kimono_details: "Deep indigo base with white camellia flower pattern, beautifully sculpted sleeves"
  hair_style: "Glossy black hair carefully styled in Japanese hair style, small kanzashi ornaments"
  posture: "Beautiful calligraphy posture sitting in seiza with extended spine"
  action: "Carefully writing characters on hanshi paper with delicate brushwork"
  focus: "Movement of brush tip and moment ink spreads on paper"
  expression: "Concentrated beautiful profile, figure's characteristic well-arranged facial features"
  scale_reference: "Scale showing miniature feel in comparison to surrounding calligraphy tools"

environment:
  setting: "Miniature-size Japanese study room set"
  surface: "Small tatami mat or wooden writing desk"
  lighting: "Soft natural light from above, beautifully reflecting contrast between ink and paper"
  atmosphere: "Quiet and sacred world of calligraphy, miniature space for concentration"
  background_elements: "Small inkstone, water dropper, paperweight, calligraphy supplies neatly arranged"
  traditional_elements: "Miniature hanging scroll, small flower arrangement, Japanese arrangement"

camera_work:
  angle: "Macro lens-style close-up shooting, close-up of brush tip and paper surface"
  focus: "Sharply capture brush movement and moment ink touches paper"
  movement: "Slow camera movement following brush flow"
  depth_of_field: "Focus on brush tip and character portions, background beautifully blurred to create atmosphere"
  scale_emphasis: "Emphasize miniature feel in comparison to calligraphy tools"

audio_emphasis:
  primary_sound: "Delicate rustling sound of brush tip touching hanshi paper"
  secondary_sound: "Subtle sound of grinding ink on inkstone, quiet sound of dipping brush in ink"
  brush_sounds: "Smooth and pleasant friction sound of brush bristles gliding on paper"
  paper_texture: "Washi paper's unique rough texture sound"
  ink_sounds: "Faint sound when ink spreads on paper"
  ambient: "Quiet study room ambient sound, occasional sound of placing brush"
  rhythm: "Rhythm of writing characters, sound changes of stops, jumps, and sweeps"
  special_notes: "Emphasize delicate sounds unique to calligraphy captured with ultra-sensitive microphone"

technical_specs:
  duration: "15-20 seconds"
  style: "Realistic, emphasizing Japanese aesthetic consciousness"
  quality: "Ultra-high definition, expressing even detailed calligraphy elements"
  motion: "Slow, elegant, and precise brushwork"
```

```yaml

  kimono_details: "Deep purple base with cherry blossom petal patterns, long sleeves sway beautifully"
  hair_style: "Glossy black hair styled elegantly in an updo, decorated with hair ornaments (kanzashi)"
  footwear: "Black leather tap shoes over tabi socks"
  action: "Rhythmic and elegant tap dance that accentuates the beauty of the kimono"
  focus: "Delicate foot movements and flowing sleeve movements of the kimono"
  expression: "Calm, focused, and beautiful expression"

  setting: "Japanese modern dance studio or large Japanese-style room"
  flooring: "Beautifully polished wooden floor (natural wood grain)"
  lighting: "Soft and elegant lighting that beautifully reflects the kimono colors"
  atmosphere: "Quiet and dignified space, environment that evokes Japanese aesthetic consciousness"
  background_elements: "Blurred Japanese-style decorations or shoji screen shadows"

  angle: "Slightly diagonal upward angle"
  focus: "Foot-centered framing"
  movement: "Slight handheld camera natural shake"
  depth_of_field: "Sharp focus on feet, background appropriately blurred"

audio_emphasis:
  primary_sound: "Elegant and resonant contact sound of tap shoes on wooden floor"
  secondary_sound: "Beautiful sound of silk kimono material rustling softly"
  sound_quality: "Clear and deep tap sound in harmony with delicate fabric sounds"
  rhythm: "Rhythm pattern incorporating Japanese aesthetic sense of 'ma' (spacing/timing)"
  ambient: "Quiet Japanese room environmental sound, occasional sound of kimono hem"

technical_specs:
  duration: "10-15 seconds"
  style: "Realistic"
  quality: "High definition"
  motion: "Smooth and natural movement"
```

## How to Use

1. **Select scene**: Choose the scene you want to generate from the three patterns
2. **Adjust parameters**: Adjust parameters within the YAML as needed
3. **Input to video generation AI**: Enter the prompt into a video generation AI like Veo3
4. **Review output**: Check the generated video

## Input Example

```text
Scene: Shodo ASMR
Adjustments: None (using default settings)
```

## Output Example

Each scene will be generated as a video containing the following elements:

**Shodo ASMR**:
- Approximately 15cm kimono figure writing characters on hanshi paper
- Delicate sound of brush tip touching paper
- Quiet and sacred atmosphere

**Origami ASMR**:
- Stunningly beautiful woman figure folding origami
- Pleasant crisp sounds of folding washi paper
- Space that feels the warmth of handcrafting

**Tap Dance ASMR**:
- Woman in kimono performing tap dance
- Elegant and resonant sound of tap shoes on wooden floor
- Japanese-modern elegant space

## Notes

- Each scene is designed as a short video of 15-25 seconds
- Assumes macro lens-style close-up shooting
- Emphasizes delicate sound descriptions to maximize ASMR effects
- Prioritizes serenity and elegance to express Japanese aesthetic consciousness (the spirit of wa)
