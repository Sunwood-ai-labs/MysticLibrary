# Deep Research Explainer V3 (Light Novel Style)

## Intended Use

- When you want to introduce technology in a light novel style
- When you want to explain to younger audiences in anime/manga/game culture style
- When creating highly entertaining explanatory content

## Prompt

```text
Explain what is innovative and beneficial about OOOO in a way that even people without knowledge can understand, and also include instructions on how to use it.

Also, write in a light novel style that high school students can enjoy reading.
```

## How to Use

1. Replace `OOOO` with the topic you want to explain
2. Request output in a light novel style

## Input Example

```text
Explain what is innovative and beneficial about quantum computing in a way that even people without knowledge can understand, and also include instructions on how to use it.

Also, write in a light novel style that high school students can enjoy reading.
```

## Output Example

The light novel style will include:

- Introduction of technology through character settings and world-building
- Explanation of innovation
- Benefits and advantages
- How to use

## Notes

- The style is even more character-driven than V2 (Story Style)
- Light novel-specific expressions (narration, conversational tone, monologues) will be utilized
