# Deep Research Brief Explainer V1

## Intended Use

- When you want to explain specialized topics to general audiences
- When you want to grasp the overview of technologies or concepts
- When you want to create explanatory content like blog articles

## Prompt

```text
Explain what is innovative and beneficial about OOOO in a way that even people without knowledge can understand, and also include instructions on how to use it.
```

## How to Use

1. Replace `OOOO` with the topic you want to explain
2. Optionally specify the target audience

## Input Example

```text
Explain what is innovative and beneficial about Deep Research in a way that even people without knowledge can understand, and also include instructions on how to use it.
```

## Output Example

The explanation will cover:

- Explanation of innovation
- Benefits and advantages
- How to use or operate

## Notes

- Since it explicitly explains in simple terms, technical terms will be accompanied by explanations as appropriate
- For complex topics, information may be simplified
