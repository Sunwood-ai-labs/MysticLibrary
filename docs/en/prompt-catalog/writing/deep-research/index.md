# Deep Research Explainers

A collection of prompts for explaining latest technologies and complex topics in an easy-to-understand way for non-experts.

## Prompt List

- [Deep Research Quick Explainer V1](./deep-research-explainer-v1.md) - Basic explanation prompt
- [Deep Research Quick Explainer V2 Story Style](./deep-research-explainer-v2-story.md) - Story-style narrative for high school students
- [Deep Research Quick Explainer V3 Light Novel Style](./deep-research-explainer-v3-light-novel.md) - Light novel style narrative
- [Deep Research Quick Explainer V3.1 Light Novel Kishotenketsu](./deep-research-explainer-v3-1-kishoutenketsu.md) - Light novel style with kishotenketsu structure

## How to Use

1. Copy the prompt and paste into AI chat tool
2. Enter the technology or topic you want to explain
3. Easy-to-understand explanation will be generated

## Features

- Explain technical terms in simple language
- Use concrete examples for clarity
- Don't assume reader's prior knowledge
- Style variations (standard, story, light novel)

## Version Selection Guide

| Version | Style | Target | Features |
|---------|-------|--------|----------|
| V1 | Standard | General adults | Simple and easy to understand |
| V2 | Story | High school students | Storytelling elements |
| V3 | Light novel | Young adults | High entertainment value |
| V3.1 | Light novel + Kishotenketsu | Young adults | Structurally complete story |
