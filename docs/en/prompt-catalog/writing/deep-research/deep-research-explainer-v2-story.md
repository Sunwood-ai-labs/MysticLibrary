# Deep Research Quick Explainer V2 Story Style

## Intended Use

- Explain technologies with story-style narrative that even high school students can enjoy reading
- Explain technology with background and stories in an approachable way
- Create easy-to-understand technical explanations for younger readers

## Prompt

```
Please explain ＯＯＯＯ in a way that even people without knowledge can understand what is innovative and what are the benefits, and also describe how to use it.

Also, make the style narrative so that even high school students can enjoy reading it.
```

## How to Use

1. Enter the above prompt into AI chat tool
2. Replace ＯＯＯＯ with the technology or topic you want to explain
3. Story-style explanation text will be generated

## Input Example

```
Please explain quantum computing in a way that even people without knowledge can understand what is innovative and what are the benefits, and also describe how to use it.

Also, make the style narrative so that even high school students can enjoy reading it.
```

## Output Example

- Story-style introduction that captures reader interest
- Background and discovery story of the technology
- Innovative points and benefits
- Specific usage methods

## Difference from V1

- Story-style narrative for more approachability
- Expressions targeted at high school students
- Added storytelling elements

## Notes

- Explain technical terms in simple language
- Include concrete examples and story elements
- Don't assume reader's prior knowledge
