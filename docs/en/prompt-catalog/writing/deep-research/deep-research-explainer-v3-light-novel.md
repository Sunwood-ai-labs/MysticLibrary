# Deep Research Quick Explainer V3 Light Novel Style

## Intended Use

- Create technical explanations with light novel style narrative
- Create approachable explanations for light novel fans and young readers
- Provide highly entertaining technical explanations

## Prompt

```
Please explain ＯＯＯＯ in a way that even people without knowledge can understand what is innovative and what are the benefits, and also describe how to use it.

Also, make the style light novel style so that even high school students can enjoy reading it.
```

## How to Use

1. Enter the above prompt into AI chat tool
2. Replace ＯＯＯＯ with the technology or topic you want to explain
3. Light novel-style explanation text will be generated

## Input Example

```
Please explain generative AI in a way that even people without knowledge can understand what is innovative and what are the benefits, and also describe how to use it.

Also, make the style light novel style so that even high school students can enjoy reading it.
```

## Output Example

- Light novel-style opening that captures readers
- Technology experience from protagonist's perspective
- Depiction of technology as a "power" or "ability"
- Innovative benefits expressed like "magic"
- Specific usage methods

## Difference from V2

- Light novel-specific expression style
- Higher entertainment value
- Game-like expressions like "cheat ability" or "skill"

## Notes

- Maintain light novel tone and expressions
- Preserve technical accuracy
- Don't assume reader's prior knowledge
