# Deep Research Quick Explainer V3.1 Light Novel Kishotenketsu

## Intended Use

- Create technical explanations with light novel-style kishotenketsu (introduction-development-turn-conclusion) structure
- Provide technical explanations conscious of story structure
- Create technical explanations that engage readers until the end

## Prompt

```
Please explain OOOO in a way that even people without knowledge can understand what is innovative and what are the benefits, and also describe how to use it.

Also, make the style light novel style so that even high school students can enjoy reading it, with conscious attention to kishotenketsu (introduction-development-turn-conclusion).
```

## How to Use

1. Enter the above prompt into AI chat tool
2. Replace OOOO with the technology or topic you want to explain
3. Light novel-style explanation with kishotenketsu structure will be generated

## Input Example

```
Please explain blockchain in a way that even people without knowledge can understand what is innovative and what are the benefits, and also describe how to use it.

Also, make the style light novel style so that even high school students can enjoy reading it, with conscious attention to kishotenketsu.
```

## Output Example

- **Ki (Introduction)**: Introduce protagonist and world setting
- **Sho (Development)**: Encounter with technology and its basic properties
- **Ten (Turn)**: Innovation and surprising features of the technology
- **Ketsu (Conclusion)**: Practical usage and future possibilities

## Difference from V3

- Clear kishotenketsu structure
- Greater sense of completion as a story
- Developments that engage readers until the end

## Notes

- Make kishotenketsu flow clear
- Maintain light novel tone and expressions
- Preserve technical accuracy
