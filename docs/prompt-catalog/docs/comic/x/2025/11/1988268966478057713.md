## Prompt Body
```text
A 4-panel gag air battle manga storyboard, horizontal layout. 
Style: childish doodle style, crayon and pencil rough sketch, minimal background (just clouds and sun). 
Tone: playful, chaotic, and full of childish energy. 
Characters: a boy (“me”) and his friend piloting their doodle fighter planes, engaged in an air battle. No dialogue, only Japanese sound words and doodle text. 
Each panel’s description and any text appearing in the image must be written in Japanese. 

Panel 1: Two doodle planes in the sky, smiling sun and “バトルスタート!!” written big. 
Panel 2: Planes shooting each other with “ババババ!!” sound effects, crooked missiles flying around. 
Panel 3: Friend’s plane smoking, escaping with doodled “まてー!!”. 
Panel 4: Big “ドカーン!!” explosion, both planes doing a friendly wing touch, smiling clouds around them. 

Global notes: keep rough uneven lines, coloring outside the lines is okay, evoke a child’s sketchbook-style dogfight. Use hand-drawn, wobbly panel borders. 
Panel numbering: draw small hand-drawn circled numbers (1–4) at the top-left inside each panel frame; uneven style for childlike tone.
```
