# Water Walk - Invisible Membrane Scene
# 水面歩行シーン

scene:
 type: cinematic video
 duration: 5-8 seconds
 aspect_ratio: 16:9

subject:
 description: young woman walking gracefully
 action: walking steadily to the left
 movement: continuous, confident stride
 expression: serene, composed

environment:
 setting: calm water surface
 time: golden hour or soft daylight
 atmosphere: ethereal, dreamlike
 water_state: still, reflective

key_elements:
 - woman walks on water surface as if walking on glass
 - feet stay completely on top of water, never breaking surface
 - feet remain dry, no water contact
 - water surface acts like solid transparent floor
 - clear reflection visible beneath her

camera:
 movement: tracking shot, following subject
 direction: lateral movement, left to right
 angle: eye level or slightly low angle
 style: smooth, steady follow

technical_notes:
 - feet NEVER sink or submerge
 - water surface remains perfectly intact
 - like walking on invisible glass platform
 - no splashing, no ripples from feet
 - maintain complete separation between feet and water

prompt: |
 Cinematic tracking shot of a young woman walking gracefully 
 on top of perfectly calm water surface, moving steadily to 
 the left. Her feet stay completely above the water as if 
 walking on an invisible glass floor, never sinking or 
 touching the water. Feet remain completely dry. Camera 
 smoothly follows her movement. Golden hour lighting, 
 ethereal atmosphere, photorealistic, 4K quality.

negative_prompt: |
 feet sinking, feet in water, wet feet, submerged, 
 splashing, ripples, water on skin, diving, swimming, 
 underwater, breaking water surface, feet touching water
