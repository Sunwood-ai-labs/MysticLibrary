🕊️ 折り鶴(鶴) – 「昇華と架け橋」
Create a hyperrealistic 3D visualization of an origami crane constructed from two-toned specialty paper in natural white and subtle gray hues, suspended elegantly over a wide monochromatic white backdrop. Emphasize tactile authenticity with fold stress marks, fine surface roughness, and delicate edge tears. Render in cinematic 16:9 horizontal format with professional soft multi-source lighting to highlight dimensional depth and paper fiber detail. Integrate an elegant typographic overlay reading "forgejo to github" in a refined modern serif font, aligned subtly at the lower center of the frame. The composition should symbolize graceful transition and bridging, prioritizing photographic precision and timeless elegance.

🦊 折り狐(キツネ) – 「知恵と変化」
Create a hyperrealistic 3D visualization of an origami fox folded from dual-toned specialty paper in warm orange and soft white, suspended elegantly over a pristine wide white backdrop. Showcase natural fold stress marks, tiny edge tears, and subtle handling impressions for realism. Frame the subject at a three-quarter perspective in cinematic 16:9 horizontal format, with soft professional lighting accentuating texture and depth. Add a sleek typographic overlay reading "forgejo to github" in an elegant italic serif font, positioned gracefully in the lower right corner. The fox should embody cleverness, transformation, and the adaptability of open-source evolution.

🦉 折りフクロウ(梟) – 「知識と洞察」
Create a hyperrealistic 3D visualization of an origami owl crafted from two-toned specialty paper in natural brown and ivory, displayed against a pure wide white backdrop. Highlight realistic material properties: fold stress lines, surface fiber detail, and delicate edge frays. Use a 16:9 horizontal format with cinematic soft studio lighting to emphasize depth and tactile authenticity. Overlay the text "forgejo to github" in a sophisticated small caps serif typeface, positioned elegantly above the lower margin in balanced spacing. The owl should symbolize wisdom, insight, and the continuity of knowledge through platforms.

🐟 折り鯉(鯉) – 「流れと力強さ」
Create a hyperrealistic 3D visualization of an origami koi fish made from dual-toned specialty paper in vibrant red and muted gold, suspended in midair over a pristine wide white background. Emphasize paper realism with fold creases, subtle fiber texture, and tiny imperfections along edges. Render in cinematic 16:9 horizontal ratio with professional soft lighting, creating dramatic dimensional shadows. Integrate an elegant typographic overlay reading "forgejo to github" in a refined serif font with subtle gold tint, aligned in the lower left corner for harmony. The koi should embody flow, resilience, and purposeful movement from one stream to another.
