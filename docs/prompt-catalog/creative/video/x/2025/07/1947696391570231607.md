Start from the provided still frame. Maintain a Hollywood-grade, vertical 9:16 commercial look: crisp highlights, controlled neon accents, gentle film grain, and shallow depth of field.

VISUAL FLOW (8–12 seconds total):
1. 0–2s | Slow push-in on the influencer’s face and phone as she starts talking; lens flares glide.
2. 2–5s | Whip-pan to reveal the full car profile; holographic spec overlays (speed, specs) float in 3D space.
3. 5–8s | Dynamic orbit around the car’s front as the hood light pulses; influencer gestures to key features.
4. 8–10s | Brief slow-motion close-up of emblem/tire spinning with light streaks.
5. 10–12s| Crash-zoom to bold end tagline over black, then a quick flash of the car again.

CAMERA & FX:
- Camera language: handheld gimbal feel with occasional buttery-smooth motion; one tasteful whip-pan; one speed-ramped crash-zoom.
- Add subtle anamorphic flares on bright highlights.
- Overlay floating UI panels (specs, price) in clean, minimal sans-serif.
- Keep transitions motivated by motion (match cuts, whip pans).

AUDIO:
- Sync to upbeat, bass-heavy electronic track. SFX: light UI beeps on spec pop-ups, a whoosh on whip-pan.

COLOR & LIGHT:
- Teal-orange base, with neon magenta accents. Preserve skin tones natural. Soft vignettes, strong contrast.

END CARD:
- Bold white tagline on black: “THE FUTURE IS IN YOUR DRIVE.” (all caps)
