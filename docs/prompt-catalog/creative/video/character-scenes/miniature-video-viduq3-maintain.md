# ViduQ3 でほっこりミニチュア動画を作成してみた

Maintain the original composition and character design. Subtle cozy motion only…

## プロンプト本文
~~~text
Maintain the original composition and character design. Subtle cozy motion only: gentle steam curls rising from the tiny mugs, warm string lights softly flickering, tiny animal friends making small natural movements (slow blink, slight head tilt, tiny breathing), raindrops sliding down the teacup rim and softly rippling the puddles outside. Very slight camera push-in, macro realism, shallow depth of field, tilt-shift preserved. No new objects, no scene changes, no text.
~~~
