# cut 1 | Establishing long shot

Japanese moe–fantasy anime, transparent watercolor style. Use wet‐on‐wet blooms…

## プロンプト本文
~~~text
Japanese moe–fantasy anime, transparent watercolor style. Use wet‐on‐wet blooms, soft bleeding, granulation/backruns, and occasional dry‐brush edges; visible rough cotton‐paper texture. Palette: ultramarine, indigo, blue‐green with faint gold dust accents. Lighting: moonlit semi‐backlight; speculars as white paper reserves.

[cut 1 | Establishing long shot]
A tranquil lake perfectly reflecting a full moon. A red torii on the shore in the foreground; a cat warrior (nekomimi) in light lamellar over a short yukata stands facing the water, cloak hem and tail lifted by a breeze. Mist rendered as broad wet‐on‐wet washes. At the warrior’s feet, tiny bell shards glint as untouched white highlights.
[cut 2 | Action medium shot]
The warrior steps knee‐deep into the lake. Ripples bleed outward as soft watercolor blooms with a crisp edge at the center. Ahead, blue‐white will‐o’‐wisps form a dotted trail (salt‐sprinkle grain). One hand rests on the katana hilt while the other reaches toward a faintly glowing “Moon Magatama” beneath the surface; the hilt bell gives a tiny jingle.
[cut 3 | Close‐up | Resolution]
The warrior brings together the magatama and a bell shard. Warm golden light diffuses outward as a watercolor bloom, illuminating the warrior’s pale face; pupils narrow like a cat’s; a calm, resolved expression. The reflected moon wavers once and settles, accompanied by a clear bell chime.
~~~
