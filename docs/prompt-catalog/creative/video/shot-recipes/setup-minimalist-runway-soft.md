# Scene Setup

&#91;Scene Setup&#93;

## プロンプト本文
~~~text
[Scene Setup]
Minimalist runway with soft lighting, seamless gradient background. 
Smooth cinematic tracking shot, camera at mid-level, slightly zooming forward. 

[Action Flow]
- Start with the first character walking toward the camera. 
- As the first character reaches center stage, they pause briefly, make a simple gesture or pose, then turn and exit gracefully toward the back of the runway. 
- Camera continues tracking forward into the empty runway space. 
- From the back of the runway, the second character enters, walking confidently toward the camera. 
- The second character makes expressive movements (head turn, hand gesture, garment flow) while walking. 
- The second character reaches center stage, slows down, then strikes a final iconic pose. 

[Style]
Chibi-style 3D vinyl toy aesthetic. 
Soft shadows, high-quality renderer output, smooth transition with continuous flow. 
Keep atmosphere stylish, elegant, and universally adaptable for any characters.
~~~
