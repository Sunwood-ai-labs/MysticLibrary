## プロンプト本文
~~~text
Ultra-fast FPV drone POV, 9:16 vertical. Night cyberpunk megacity, rain-slick streets, neon signs, holograms, steam vents. The drone slaloms between skyscrapers, dives through a narrow alley, threads under skybridges, then rockets upward past glowing billboards. Cinematic, crisp, high shutter feel (minimal motion smear), stable horizon, aggressive banking turns, realistic speed parallax, lens distortion subtle. Rain droplets on lens for 1–2 beats, reflections streaking on glass. End on a rooftop flyby with city depth.
 BGM: high-BPM synthwave / cyber techno (140–160 BPM), punchy kick, sidechained bass, bright arpeggios, occasional riser before each dive, clean modern mix。
~~~
