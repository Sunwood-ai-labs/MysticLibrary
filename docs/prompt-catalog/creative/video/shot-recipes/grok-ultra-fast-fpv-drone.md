## プロンプト本文
~~~text
Ultra-fast FPV drone POV at night, Japanese touge time-attack. The drone flies 10–20cm above the asphalt, extreme speed sensation, wide FOV but stable horizon, high shutter clarity (almost no smear). A tuned sports car is full attack, late braking into hairpins, instant throttle on exit, minimal drift—pure grip aggression. The drone stays glued to the rear bumper, then slingshots to the inside apex, skimming the guardrail by centimeters, sparks from tiny gravel hits, road reflectors (cats-eyes) strobing past like bullets. Rapid micro elevation changes, compression and rebound over bumps, audible turbo spool + wastegate, engine screaming.
 
BGM: hard techno / rave (155–165 BPM), relentless kick, driving offbeat bass, short risers into every corner entry, sharp crash on apex, energetic “drop” immediately。
~~~
