~~~text

The DeLorean begins to glow with blue neon energy, panels shifting and folding mechanically, gullwing doors rotating upward to form shoulder armor, tires retracting into armored legs, cinematic sparks and steam bursting out, dramatic transformation into a towering humanoid mecha, hyper-detailed, slow-motion cinematic sequence

2 ロボット → 戦闘機
The humanoid mecha compresses and reconfigures, arms retracting and locking into wing-like structures, chest plating splitting open to reveal glowing thrusters, gullwing armor unfolding into aerodynamic stabilizers, bright energy bursts ignite as the machine transforms into a sleek futuristic fighter jet, cinematic aerial transition, photorealistic with glowing trails
~~~
