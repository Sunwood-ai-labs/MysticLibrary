# 私もKling2.1 のフレーム指定機能で作ってみた!!

🦊 Kitsune ➝ 👺 Tengu

## プロンプト本文
~~~text
🦊 Kitsune ➝ 👺 Tengu
The fox made from the letters of "KITSUNE" spreads its tails, each tail composed of curling letters. The tails dissolve into drifting ash-like fragments of the word, carried upward by a sudden storm wind. Those fragments swirl and reshape into wings, while elongated letters stretch forward to form a sharp nose and fan, revealing the "TENGU" silhouette rising from misty currents.

👺 Tengu ➝ 👹 Oni
The Tengu flaps its letter-formed wings, scattering "TENGU" fragments like feathers across the scene. These fragments crash to the ground, glowing red-hot as cracks appear beneath them. From the fissures, molten letters of "ONI" erupt, hammering themselves into horns and a muscular frame, until the towering Oni stands fully formed from the glowing typography.

👹 Oni ➝ 🌀 Rokurokubi
The Oni slams its weapon, and its entire body shatters into sharp glowing shards of the word "ONI". The shards scatter outward like an explosive shockwave, then fade into ripples across a dark water surface. Out of these ripples, elongated letters of "ROKUROKUBI" rise like serpents, twisting higher and higher into a long neck, finally forming the pale face above a kimono-shaped cascade of flowing text.

🌀 Rokurokubi ➝ 🦊 Kitsune
The Rokurokubi’s neck of elongated "ROKUROKUBI" letters stretches endlessly into the void, twisting and thinning until the letters unravel into streams of mist. As the pale face fades, the drifting fragments scatter downward like falling snow. Suddenly, sparks ignite within the fragments, the drifting letters glowing orange and curling into flame. The fragments swirl together, tails forming first, then pointed ears and sharp eyes—until the fiery silhouette of a Kitsune is reborn from the blazing letters of "KITSUNE".
~~~
