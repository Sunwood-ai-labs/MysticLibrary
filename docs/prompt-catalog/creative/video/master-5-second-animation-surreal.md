## プロンプト本文
~~~text
Create a 5-second animation of a surreal glass apple sitting on a stormy shoreline. Inside the apple, the ocean waves ripple gently, crashing against the miniature tree rooted in the center. Outside, lightning bolts illuminate the stormy sky in quick flashes, with the thunder subtly shaking the scene. The water within the apple swirls dynamically, reflecting the turbulent waves of the real ocean in perfect harmony. Add small droplets of water dripping down the apple’s surface, as if it’s absorbing the storm around it. The camera slowly pans around the apple to showcase the interplay between the serene inner world and the chaotic storm outside, ending with a powerful flash of lightning illuminating both worlds simultaneously.
~~~
