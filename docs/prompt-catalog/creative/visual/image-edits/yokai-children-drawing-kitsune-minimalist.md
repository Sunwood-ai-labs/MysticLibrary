# Children's drawing プロンプトで妖怪を作ってみた!!

1. Kitsune(狐)

## プロンプト本文
~~~text
1. Kitsune(狐)

💬Prompt:
A Kitsune in a minimalist children's drawing style, with flowing multiple tails drawn in thick white glowing lines. The background shows a softly blurred moonlit forest. Floating elements like tiny glowing fireballs and falling leaves add a whimsical touch. Full-body view, warm and simple aesthetic.

2. Tengu(天狗)

💬Prompt:
A Tengu in a minimalist children's drawing style, its long nose and feathered wings outlined in thick white glowing lines. The softly blurred background suggests a mountain shrine at dusk. Floating elements like drifting feathers and glowing maple leaves bring a playful feel. Full-body view, warm and simple aesthetic.

3. Rokurokubi(ろくろ首)

💬Prompt:
A Rokurokubi in a minimalist children's drawing style, with a stretched long neck curving playfully in thick glowing white lines. The background is softly blurred with a quiet Japanese village under lantern light. Floating elements like glowing lanterns and moths in the night sky add a whimsical charm. Full-body view, warm and simple aesthetic.

4. Oni(鬼)

💬Prompt:
An Oni in a minimalist children's drawing style, its horns and muscular body drawn with glowing thick white lines. The softly blurred background depicts a misty mountain cave. Floating elements like glowing sparks and drifting smoke make the scene lively yet gentle. Full-body view, warm and simple aesthetic.
~~~
