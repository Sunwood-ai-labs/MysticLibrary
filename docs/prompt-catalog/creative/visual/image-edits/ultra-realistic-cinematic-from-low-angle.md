## プロンプト本文
~~~text
Ultra-realistic cinematic shot from a low-angle perspective **on the blazing photosphere of the Sun**. A 1960 red Ford Mustang rests on the roiling granulation, its chrome igniting molten-gold reflections, while **a turquoise 1960 Chevrolet Corvette races along the crest of a towering looping solar prominence** in the mid-distance. Gigantic plasma cells curve dramatically into the foreground and sweep upward toward incandescent prominence arches in the background, emphasizing the Sun’s titanic scale. No planets or rings—only the continuous, turbulent solar surface and prominences physically attached to the star. Deep space with faint stars frames the scene beyond the corona. Hyper-realistic lighting: searing glare highlights metallic car surfaces and refractive sparks shimmer through super-heated plasma. Shot on 70 mm IMAX film with a subtle surreal filter; scatter a handful of well-dressed Parisians from 1960 near the Mustang and add vintage street-lights whose glass bulbs glow fiercely under the solar fire.
~~~
