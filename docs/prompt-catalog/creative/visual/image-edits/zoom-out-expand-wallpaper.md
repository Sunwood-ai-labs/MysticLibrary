# 編集契約書:16:9アウトペイント

Zoom out and expand the image to 16:9 wallpaper.

## プロンプト本文
~~~text
TASK:
Zoom out and expand the image to 16:9 wallpaper.

PRESERVE:
Original center content remains untouched (no distortion, no resizing artifacts).

FILL LOGIC:
Extend scenery left/right as a natural continuation.
Complete cut-off objects at borders with logical geometry.

MATCH:
Lighting, weather, texture, grain, focus depth must match the original.

DO NOT:
No repetitive patterns, no blurry smears, no mismatched perspective.
~~~
