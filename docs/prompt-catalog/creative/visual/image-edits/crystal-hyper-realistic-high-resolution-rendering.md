## プロンプト本文
~~~text
A hyper-realistic high-resolution rendering of a towering slice of strawberry shortcake sculpted entirely from translucent rose-quartz crystal. Smooth, glossy layers refract incoming light with a radiant, candy-glass glow, while embedded crystal strawberries pulse like rubies beneath the surface. Ultra-fine details reveal airy sponge textures frozen in glass, peppered with micro-bubbles that sparkle like diamonds. The dessert rests on a pale blush background, surrounded by crisp shadows and luminous pink caustics that pool like liquid light. Studio spotlights skim every facet, amplifying reflections and scattering pastel hues across the scene. The mood is luxurious, dreamy, and minimal, blending nostalgic patisserie charm with high-gloss surrealism. Style: ultra-detailed, hyper-realistic glass rendering, still life, cinematic studio lighting, high-resolution minimalism.
~~~
