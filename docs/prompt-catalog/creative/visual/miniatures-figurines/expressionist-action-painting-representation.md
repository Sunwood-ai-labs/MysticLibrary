# EXPRESSIONIST ACTION PAINTING プロンプトを私も使ってみた!!

An Expressionist Action Painting representation of a lone figure wandering through a rain-soaked midnight cit…

## プロンプト本文
~~~text
1.

An Expressionist Action Painting representation of **a lone figure wandering through a rain-soaked midnight city**, with dynamic, spontaneous gestures and paint splatters. Employ energetic strokes of **electric blue** and **neon magenta** to convey raw emotion and physicality. **Overlay the title “Neon Solitude” in an elegant high-contrast serif (Didot/Bodoni style), small caps with gentle tracking, placed bottom-center; use soft **electric blue** at 80% opacity with a subtle glow for legibility.**

2.

An Expressionist Action Painting representation of **a lighthouse resisting a storm-tossed sea**, with dynamic, spontaneous gestures and paint splatters. Employ energetic strokes of **deep green** and **titanium white** to convey raw emotion and physicality. **Overlay the title “Storm-Born” in an elegant serif (Didot/Bodoni style), medium weight, upper/lowercase, placed top-left; set in **titanium white** with slight letterspacing to complement the spray.**

3.

An Expressionist Action Painting representation of **a dancer striking a drum in mid-motion**, with dynamic, spontaneous gestures and paint splatters. Employ energetic strokes of **carmine red** and **saffron yellow** to convey raw emotion and physicality. **Overlay the title “Pulse of the Drum” in an elegant serif, italic, placed bottom-right following the motion arc; set in **carmine red** with a thin **saffron yellow** inline for a refined accent.**

4.

An Expressionist Action Painting representation of **an ancient cherry tree remembering past blossoms**, with dynamic, spontaneous gestures and paint splatters. Employ energetic strokes of **indigo** and **burnished copper** to convey raw emotion and physicality. **Overlay the title “Memory of the Old Cherry Tree” in an elegant serif, light weight with generous tracking, centered near the top; set in **burnished copper** with minimal shadow for contrast against indigo.**
~~~
