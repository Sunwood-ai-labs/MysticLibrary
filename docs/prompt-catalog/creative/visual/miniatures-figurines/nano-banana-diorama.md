# NANO BANANAでGoogleマップ画像から水槽都市ジオラマ化にしてみた! これは持ち運びたい!!!

Create a photorealistic isometric diorama inside a modern transparent suitcase.

## プロンプト本文
~~~text
Create a photorealistic isometric diorama inside a modern transparent suitcase. 
Use the provided map of Nagoya Port Aquarium as the base: 
preserve the recognizable coastline, roads, plazas, pathways, green parks, and the distinctive shapes of the aquarium building, dolphin pool, penguin zone, and surrounding facilities. 

The terrain should remain as miniature landmasses and plazas, but all of it is submerged in crystal-clear blue seawater inside the suitcase. 
Landmarks should keep their real-world outlines, like the main pool, paths, and buildings, but adapted into detailed diorama-style models. 

Add realistic aquatic life: dolphins leaping in the dolphin pool, penguins swimming in their zone, schools of fish circling the plaza areas, jellyfish drifting above pathways, coral reefs around the coastlines, and rays gliding over the map terrain. 
Keep the ground textures visible through the water, with sand, stone, and greenery preserved, giving the impression of a submerged city map.

Highlight photorealism with cinematic underwater lighting: sunlight rays penetrating the water, bubbles rising, and shimmering caustic reflections on both the terrain and the transparent glass walls of the suitcase. 
The suitcase should clearly frame the miniature map as a portable water-tank city, ultra-detailed, HDR, cinematic look.
~~~
