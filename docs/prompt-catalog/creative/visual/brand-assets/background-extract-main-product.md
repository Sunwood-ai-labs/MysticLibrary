# 編集契約書:白背景EC物撮りに再構成

Extract the main product and recreate a premium e-commerce product shot on pure white background.

## プロンプト本文
~~~text
TASK:
Extract the main product and recreate a premium e-commerce product shot on pure white background.

PRESERVE (DO NOT CHANGE):
Product shape, logo, printed text, material feel, true colors.

REMOVE / MODIFY ONLY:
Hands, clutter, messy background.

LIGHTING:
Soft studio light, even exposure. Add a subtle natural contact shadow under the product.

DO NOT:
No warped labels, no smudgy inpainting, no gray background, no halos.
~~~
