~~~text
“Create a hyper-realistic 1080x1080 square render of a large, fur-covered werewolf hand with sharp claws gently holding a rounded, beveled miniature display platform showcasing a 3D collectible diorama of an 'AI Werewolf Village'. Feature rustic, gothic European-style cottages intermingled with futuristic glowing data cables, small server monoliths, and cyborg-like werewolf totems. A central clock tower has a glowing digital eye motif. Miniature dense, dark forests surround the village. A bold 3D sign reading 'AI人狼村' (with 'AI WEREWOLF VILLAGE' subtitle) is cleanly built into the front edge of the platform, glowing faintly with blue light. Use a moody, dark color scheme dominated by deep forest greens, rustic browns, and stone grays, accented by cool blue and purple LED glows from the AI technology elements. Matte textures for the village contrast with realistic fur and claw textures. Light the scene with dramatic, low-key studio illumination; warm highlights touch the fur, while cool light emanates from the village's AI elements, creating deep, atmospheric shadows. Place the composition against a dark, neutral gray gradient backdrop, keeping the same viewing angle and perspective for consistency. Add atmospheric fog around the miniature base, photorealistic textures, and ultra-precise detailing for an 8K quality high-end collectible aesthetic”
