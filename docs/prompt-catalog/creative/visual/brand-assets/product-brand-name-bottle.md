# ブリーフ型:高級プロダクト広告

PRODUCT:

## プロンプト本文
~~~text
PRODUCT:
[BRAND] [PRODUCT NAME] — bottle shape: [SHAPE], label: [LABEL], liquid: [COLOR]

SCENE:
Product floating just above dark water. Surround with [FLOWER TYPE] in [COLOR PALETTE].
Realistic ripples and reflections.

LIGHTING:
Choose: “golden-hour glow” or “bright fresh studio light”.
Keep reflections physically plausible.

STYLE:
High-end commercial photography, shallow depth of field, premium retouch (not plastic).

CONSTRAINTS:
Label text crisp, logo not warped, no unrealistic liquid behavior.
~~~
