# High-resolution, clean, and polished anime style.

## プロンプト本文
~~~text
Create a group photo using the three provided character reference images. 
Arrange all characters standing side by side, facing the camera, with friendly smiles. 
Unify the art style to anime illustration with consistent linework and color palette. 
Make the background a bright outdoor park with blue sky and greenery. 
Ensure proportions are correct, no distortions, and all characters look naturally photographed together. 
High-resolution, clean, and polished anime style.
~~~
