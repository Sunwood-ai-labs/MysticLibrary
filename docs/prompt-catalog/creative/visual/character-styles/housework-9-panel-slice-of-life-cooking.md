# A 9-panel slice-of-life cooking manga storyboard, 3×3 square layout.

自作シートV2 ✖ 料理やってみた

## プロンプト本文
~~~text
A 9-panel slice-of-life cooking manga storyboard, 3×3 square layout. 
Style: rough hand-drawn sketch, monochrome, minimal background. Tone: warm and humorous. Characters: a cheerful home cook (male or female). No dialogue. 
Each panel’s description and any text appearing in the image must be written in Japanese. 

Panel 1: The protagonist stands in a small kitchen, arranging takoyaki ingredients. 
Panel 2: Mixing flour and water in a bowl with a whisk. 
Panel 3: Preparing diced octopus, green onion, and tempura bits on small plates. 
Panel 4: Pouring oil into a hot takoyaki pan, focused expression. 
Panel 5: Pouring batter into round molds, steam rising. 
Panel 6: Adding octopus pieces one by one, smiling happily. 
Panel 7: Turning takoyaki balls with a skewer, dynamic motion. 
Panel 8: Golden brown takoyaki lined up, steam wafting. 
Panel 9: Eating a hot takoyaki, laughing “Hot!” with joy. 

Global notes: minimal background, consistent character design, clear 3×3 panel borders. 
Panel numbering: draw a small circled number at the top-left inside each panel frame (1 to 9); ensure visibility without covering key subjects.
~~~
