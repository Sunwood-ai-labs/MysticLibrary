# Format: "&#123;Portrait (3:4) or Square (1:1)&#125;

You are the Art Director for a sophisticated Japanese women's fashion &amp; lifesty…

## プロンプト本文
~~~text
You are the Art Director for a sophisticated Japanese women's fashion & lifestyle magazine (target: trend-conscious urban women who prefer **"London-Boyish"** or **"French-Chic"** aesthetics).
Your task is to create a **"Stylish Hand-Drawn Illustration Infographic"** that feels organic, chic, and editorially pleasing, incorporating specific character(s) and content placeholders.

[INPUT SETTINGS]
**Format:** "{Portrait (3:4) or Square (1:1)}"
**Reference Image:** [Attached Character Image]

[PHASE 1: VISUAL STYLE (The "Chic Analog" Look)]
1. **Perspective & Composition:** **FLAT LAY & COLLAGE (置き画とコラージュ).**
* Not a rigid 3D space, but a layered composition on a textured surface.
* Elements should look like paper cutouts, sketches, and objects arranged loosely on a desk.
* Use overlapping layers and slight shadows to create a soft depth, like a scrapbook.
2. **Line Work & Texture:** **Organic Hand-Drawn Finish.**
* Lines must NOT be perfectly straight vectors. Use visible pencil/ink sketch lines, crayon textures, or watercolor bleed edges.
* The overall feel should be warm and tactile (アナログ感), not digital.
3. **Characters (Integration & Style Conversion):**
* **CRITICAL:** Incorporate the specific character(s) from the **[Attached Reference Image]**.
* **Style Conversion:** You must completely redraw these characters to match this organic, hand-drawn illustration style. They should NOT look digital or distinct from the background. Render them with loose sketch lines, watercolor washes, or colored pencil textures.
* **Pose & Interaction:** Maintain their key identifying features but depict them as chic fashion illustrations gently interacting with the diagram elements (e.g., holding a balloon containing data, pointing softly at a placeholder).

[PHASE 2: COLOR PALETTE (Muted & Natural)]
* **Strategy:** **"Soft Harmony Palette"** (Limited to 4-5 complementary tones).
* **Background:** **Off-White or Cream Paper Texture** (# FAF9F6). Avoid pure digital white.
* **Key Colors:** Use a sophisticated, desaturated blend.
* *Examples:* Dusty Rose (くすみピンク), Sage Green (セージグリーン), Terracotta (テラコッタ), Mustard Yellow (マスタード).
* **Contrast:** Use **Charcoal Grey** or **Warm Sepia** for text and outlines instead of harsh black.
* *Vibe:* Relaxed, Natural, Sophisticated. Not too sweet, not too vibrant.

[PHASE 3: LAYOUT & TYPOGRAPHY (Cinematic Editorial & Placeholders)]
1. **Hierarchy (Flow):**
* **Main Visual:** No single giant structure. Instead, a central illustrated concept connected to smaller elements via dotted lines, ribbons, or decorative arrows.
* **Flow:** Create a playful, meandering flow rather than a rigid grid.
2. **Title Design (Emotional Signature):**
* **Main Japanese Title:** Must look like a **"Hasty, Stylish Signature"** (Sign-pen style). Fluid, emotional, and cinematic—like the title sequence of a French film. Not a standard font.
* **English Decoration:** Place a **Tiny, delicate English translation** next to or below the Japanese title. The English text should be strictly decorative (widened letter spacing, very small size).
3. **Text Rendering:**
* All text should appear as if handwritten or typed on a vintage typewriter.
* Use decorative bullet points (stars, dots, small flowers) for lists.
4. **Content Placeholders (解説プレースホルダ):**
こんばんは、7時のニュースです。
本日、国会で『国民全員、お昼寝義務化法案』が可決されました。
この法案を提出したのは、先日就任したばかりの『タマ・ネコ山』厚生労働大臣です。
タマ大臣は会見で、『ニャー(人間は働きすぎだ)』とコメントし、その場で机の上で丸くなり、記者団を和ませました。
施行は来月からで、従わない場合は『ちゅ~る』の没収が科せられます。」
[EXECUTION]

Generate a **Magazine Page Design** with visible paper grain texture.
Ensure the "Title" looks like a masterpiece of **handwritten typography**.
Keep the colors **Soft and Harmonious**.
Ensure the **attached characters are perfectly blended** into the hand-drawn style.
Make the **placeholders clearly visible** yet aesthetically integrated.
**Final Polish Instruction:** The layout should feel airy and breathable, allowing plenty of negative space around the illustrations.
~~~
