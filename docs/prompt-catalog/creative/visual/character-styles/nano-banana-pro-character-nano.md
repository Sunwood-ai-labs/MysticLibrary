# nano banana pro で ドット絵GIFに挑戦中❽

Flat pixel art style with zero shading.

## プロンプト本文
~~~text
GENERAL STYLE & MOOD:
Flat pixel art style with zero shading.
Strict 16-color limited palette.
Pure solid color fills (No dithering, no gradients, no texture).
Sharp, distinct pixels.

SUBJECT:
Animal sprites (e.g., foxes, cats, rabbits) arranged in a clean grid.
Simple, readable silhouettes suitable for animation frames.
Side-view or front-view orientation.

COMPOSITION:
Aspect Ratio 7:4 (Horizontal layout).
Evenly spaced grid arrangement for easy GIF slicing.
Consistent sprite size.

LIGHTING & COLOR:
No lighting source (Unlit / Fullbright).
Completely flat coloring with no shadows or highlights.
Vibrant colors that pop against the background.

ENVIRONMENT:
Pure Black Background (#000000).
High contrast separation between subject and background.

CONSTRAINTS:
No anti-aliasing.
No blurry edges.
No mixed colors or soft transitions.
No complex details or noise.
~~~
