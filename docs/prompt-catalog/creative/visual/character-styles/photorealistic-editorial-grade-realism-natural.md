# ブリーフ型:大人数・シネマティック集合写真

Photorealistic, editorial-grade realism, natural skin texture, subtle film grain.

## プロンプト本文
~~~text
GENERAL STYLE & MOOD:
Photorealistic, editorial-grade realism, natural skin texture, subtle film grain.

SUBJECT:
A lively group portrait with 25–40 people of varied ages and outfits, interacting naturally.
Everyone looks distinct.

COMPOSITION:
Slightly wide 35mm look, cinematic framing, shallow depth of field.

LIGHTING:
Soft ambient fill + warm rim light from one side (sunset feel). Accurate shadows/reflections.

ENVIRONMENT:
Rooftop terrace at golden hour, modern skyline bokeh, polished floor highlights.

CONSTRAINTS:
No duplicated limbs, no melted faces, no warped jewelry, no random text.
~~~
