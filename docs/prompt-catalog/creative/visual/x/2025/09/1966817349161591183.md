## Prompt Body
```text
Create a typographic art portrait themed around the mysterious **world of Japanese yokai**. In the center, depict a surreal, dreamlike figure: a shape-shifting yokai with glowing eyes, flowing kimono-like patterns, and ghostly ornaments. Their presence should feel both alluring and unsettling — a being halfway between human and spirit. Surround them with floating lanterns, smoke, and shadowy silhouettes of other yokai (tengu, kappa, kitsune tails, one-eyed creatures). The expression should be enigmatic, mischievous, and otherworldly.

The entire composition should be overlaid with **Japanese typography** that follows the contours of their face, horns, hair, and spirit flames. Typography must feel alive — twisting like smoke, dripping like ink, cutting across the glowing eyes — blending with the image like a curse written in the air.

### HUGE TEXT (dominating the image, partially cut off at edges):

* 「妖怪百鬼夜行」
* 「闇に潜む真実」
* 「異界の饗宴」

### MEDIUM TEXT (floating around the portrait, wrapping around hair and kimono):

* 「平安の影より現る」
* 「永遠に彷徨う魂」
* 「妖しき契約」
* 「人と怪の境界」
* 「月下の面霊気」

### SMALL TEXT (filling spaces like whispers, charms, talismans):

* 「狐火」 「河童」 「一つ目小僧」 「ろくろ首」 「輪入道」 「土蜘蛛」 「影踏み」 「呪詛」 「怪談」 「霊灯」 「憑依」 「怪異」 「物の怪」 「祟り」

### SURPRISING REVELATIONS (medium/large text, integrated into chaos):

* 「実は人の夢を食らって生きている」
* 「姿は見る者ごとに変わる」
* 「語る言葉はすべて呪い」
* 「誰も知らぬうちにそばにいる」
* 「昼は町に紛れ、夜は怪に戻る」
* 「人間の記憶を盗んで棲んでいる」

Typography must be **white, high-contrast, wildly varied in size and weight**, almost chaotic. Some characters should stretch beyond the frame, others should curl like smoke or blood veins. The integration of text and image should create a **chaotic, energetic, haunting atmosphere**, evoking the feeling of stepping into the **妖怪の世界**.
```
