~~~text
Ultra-fast FPV drone POV in a minimal white infinite studio (cyclorama), 16:9. No scenery—only speed cues: floating black lines, geometric hoops, and particles that stretch into elegant motion trails as the drone passes. The drone performs impossible-feeling but physically plausible high-G turns, abrupt altitude changes, and razor-straight sprints; camera remains stable and clean, hyper-precise tracking, no wobble. Visual language is abstract: streaks, parallax grids, strobing markers timed to beats. End on a sudden full stop with particles settling.
 
BGM: minimal techno / experimental electronic (128–132 BPM), metronomic kick, sparse clicks, evolving sub-bass, wide reverb hits; occasional “beat cut” during the full stop, then a final deep boom。
~~~
