📒 1. The Power Shirt

Prompt:
Create a striking typographic art portrait of a modern woman wearing a crisp oversized white shirt, styled off-shoulder with dramatic cuffs. She stands against a minimalist editorial backdrop, bathed in soft sunlight and geometric shadows. The shirt fabric should appear ultra-detailed — cotton textures, subtle folds, and a slightly undone elegance.

Overlay the image with bold, high-fashion typography in matte black and pale gold, wrapping around the shirt’s contours, curving across her collarbone, and slicing through the shadow lines.

* HUGE TEXT (edge-cut, magazine cover energy):
 “THE SHIRT REBORN”
* MEDIUM TEXT (floating across fabric folds):
 “Effortless power” / “Casual turned couture” / “The new uniform”
* SMALL TEXT (like whispers in the margins):
 “silhouette” / “folds” / “breathable” / “chic” / “timeless”

📒 3. The Modern Skirt

 “Structure meets freedom” / “Urban armor” / “The silhouette of tomorrow”
* SMALL TEXT (scattered like sparks):
 “neon” / “city” / “edge” / “nightfall” / “movement”
